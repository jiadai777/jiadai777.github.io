About Us

My name is Jason Liu and I am a recent SFSU graduate who holds a Master's Degree in English from SFSU and a Bachelor's Degree in Managerial Economics from UC Davis. I am a part-time instructor at Skyline College. Throughout my secondary and post-secondary education, I tutored and taught many students of different ages, classes, and subjects. I want to expand my teaching career by developing my own unique teaching style and curriculum.

The classroom will be in a spacious room at my house where I live with my parents. Since I just started this home business, I strive our best to build up my reputation, therefore I am offering low prices to attract more students. As I gain more capital resources, I will gradually expand and enhance my educational spaces and facilities, providing better and better educational experiences to more and more students.

All in all, you or your children's educational growth is also my growth, so let's work together along the journey!

What We Do

We provide professional one-on-one and group tutoring for secondary school students and adults. We teach in popular subjects such as English, mathematics, and major test preps.
